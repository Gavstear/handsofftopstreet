Cameron Homes

Cameron Homes is a Midlands-based housebuilder known for targeting semi-rural and village-edge locations. While they promote themselves as a quality developer, past experiences have raised concerns about their reliability and accountability.

In a previous development at Lea Castle Village (Kidderminster), Cameron Homes was served with a Temporary Stop Notice by the local authority due to unauthorised construction activities and a failure to deliver essential flood mitigation works. This breach undermined public trust and resulted in complaints from residents about inadequate consultation and disruption during the build phase.

Their current proposal for Appleby Magna has also raised serious questions about transparency, given that the first public notice arrived via leaflet just days before a limited-time drop-in exhibition, providing minimal opportunity for meaningful engagement.

Evolve Planning

Evolve Planning is a consultancy firm that works with developers across the UK. They specialise in securing planning permissions for edge-of-settlement and greenfield sites, often in areas where development is contentious.
