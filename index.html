Protecting Our Village, Preserving Our Countryside

Welcome to the official campaign site opposing the proposed housing development off Top Street, Appleby Magna. We are a community-led group of residents dedicated to safeguarding our village’s character, history, and precious green spaces from irreversible development.

What's Happening?

Cameron Homes, in collaboration with Evolve Planning, is proposing to build around 50 homes on open countryside directly behind Didcott Way. This site has previously been deemed unsuitable for development by the local council — yet developers are back, pushing ahead with minimal consultation and vague promises.

Why We’re Fighting Back

  • ✅ The site lies outside the village’s designated development area
  • ✅ Harmful impact on heritage and landscape
  • ✅ Poor infrastructure and service support
  • ✅ Wildlife habitats and open views under threat
  • ✅ Developer trust and transparency concerns

This site is not allocated for development under the Local Plan. Once our countryside is lost, it's lost forever. We believe in sustainable planning, not reckless sprawl.
